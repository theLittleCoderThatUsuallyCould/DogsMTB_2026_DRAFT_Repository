Beginning of the Season


There is a spring kick-off meeting. Join the mailing list so you don’t miss it.

The first practice is typically the first Tuesday after Memorial Day. Join the mailing list and Slack to get up-to-date information!



Practices are USUALLY:



TUESDAYS:



Island Lake







Meet at the pavilion at the end of the road , or ride in from the JCPenney Parking lot .





THURSDAYS:



Brighton Recreation Area






Meet at the pavilion nearest the parking lot.





PRACTICE TIMES:


5:00 PM


Meet
Take attendance
Assemble into riding groups



5:15 PM


Get on the trail to ride (individual group ride time may be slightly later)



7:30 PM (or there about)


Practice ends




Notes:


* Slack is used to communicate any changes to this, either different times, locations or if practice is canceled.

* Weekend rides are optional and are held at varying locations. Check Slack.

*A Michigan State Parks Recreation Passport is needed to park at both practice locations.

* The last practice of the season is the Thursday before the last MiSCA race of the season, in October.

* There are 6 MiSCA Races, which start in August, and are a great way to show how each rider has grown throughout the season.
