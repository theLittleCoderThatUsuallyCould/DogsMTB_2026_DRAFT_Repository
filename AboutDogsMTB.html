History

Brighton Cycling was started in 2015 by Principal Johnson of the Brighton High School. There were only 25 student athletes that first year.
In 2025 Brighton Cycling had an active team roster of over 58 student athletes, ranging from K to 12th grade.

MiSCA



MiSCA empowers communities to develop and grow K-12 youth mountain biking programs across the state.

They instill confidence by promoting the values of an active lifestyle, healthy competition, and outdoor stewardship and adventure while building life-long friendships in an inclusive environment.

They provide skills camps and clinics, as well as coaching curriculum, guidance, and certification.


MiSCA Races



Michigan Scholastic Cycling Association puts on 6 youth-only races in the fall. These are not mandatory (unless you are trying to letter in high school), but we do suggest athletes try at least one to get the full experience.
